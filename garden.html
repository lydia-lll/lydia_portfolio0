Garden: Eternity

An Chinese-painting-style immersive environment, where time slows down

Garden: Eternity


This immersive experience offers a nuanced exploration of temporal perception, leveraging Chinese ink-inspired garden design to create a multilayered spatial landscape that engages with the notion of time as malleable and dynamic. The integration of traditional Chinese garden elements and labyrinthine structure fosters a sense of meditative immersion, with ever-burning incense serving as a potent symbol of the spiritual and physical realms. By providing opportunities for engagement with the virtual creatures within the garden, the experience facilitates a deeper connection with the environment and encourages contemplation of the interconnectedness of time and space.

  • Form: VR immersive Environment
  • Project Type: Individual Work
  • My Role: Designer & Developer
  • Tools in Use: Unity, Blender, Oculus Quest2
  • Completed on: 15 Oct 2023
