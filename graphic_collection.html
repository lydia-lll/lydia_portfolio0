Beyond the Frame

Part of the Chinese-painting-inspired NFT series created during my internship at OUP

Inspired by paintings of Shi Tao

Drawing inspiration from the Buddhist allegory 'One Flower, One World, One Leaf, One Bodhi,' this animated series breathes life into the painted worlds. In the artwork of Shi Tao, the dynamic gaze of the Arhat engages with viewers, seemingly peering through the screen, creating an interactive experience.

In this moment, are we, the observers, appreciating the world within the painting, or is the figure within the artwork gazing back at us?
