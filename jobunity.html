JOBunity


In China, 85 million individuals with disabilities struggle to find employment and social inclusion. Despite their eagerness to work, existing platforms fail to address their unique needs. Our mission is to bridge this gap by creating an inclusive platform that connects them with job opportunities and fosters a supportive community.

  • Project Type: Group Project
