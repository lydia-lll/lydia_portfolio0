Dynamic Zero


Dynamic Zero, a short film produced in the spring of 2022, captures the haunting atmosphere of the Shanghai lockdown and the pervasive surveillance culture that pervaded it. The film utilizes eyes as a metaphor to convey the feeling of being watched and controlled, and powerfully conveys the tension between government policies and the frustration and anger felt by the people who were forced to endure them. It is a poignant commentary on the impact of authoritarianism and the struggle for freedom.

  • Form: Short film
  • Project Type: Group Project
  • My Role: Storyboard, Audio & Video Editing
  • Tools in Use: Adobe Audition, Premiere Pro, Final Cut Pro, ArcTime Pro
  • Completed on: 2022
