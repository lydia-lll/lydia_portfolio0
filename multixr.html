Verse: No. 1555 - an immersive digital journey through collective NYUSH memories of Century Avenue 1555

Verse No.1555


Verse: No. 1555 is a virtual journey through the collective recollections of the NYUSH community, encapsulating the living history of our old campus, Century Avenue 1555. The web-based VR and mobile AR portal creates a dynamic, interactive space that invites users to explore the virtual building and discover shared memories left by others. By digitizing personal remembrances, this project honors the significance of the physical structure as a locus of individual and communal experiences, evoking an emotional resonance that transcends the physical boundaries of the building.

Final production was exhibited at NYU Shanghai 10th Anniversary & TEDx program.

  • Form: XR experience (web VR + AR)
  • Project Type: Group Project
  • Supervisor: Prof. Zhang, Xingchen
  • My Role: Technical Artist & Programmer
  • Tools in Use: Unity, PhotoShop, EasyAR, Vuforia
  • Completed on: 2022




Problem Statement

This project wants to focus on the seemingly inconsequential, yet essential moments of everyday life that collectively form the rich fabric of human experience.

These 'non-essential' stories, often overlooked, are what truly shape our lives and the fabric of our collective history.
