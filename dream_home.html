True Dream Home


It's time to explore UX design in the XR landscape â€” in True Dream Home, we collaborated with our client, game studio Fun-gi, to elevate the game House Flip's potential and introduce VR to their current demographic. In True Dream Home, players are able to bring their dream home design in House Flip to the virtual space. Blending with the concept of virtual travel, this project immerses users in VR to experience dream homes in desired locations.

  • Form: VR Experience Design
  • Project Type: Group Project
  • My Role: Developer
  • Tools in Use: Unity, Figma
  • Completed on: 27 April 2023




Our Client: Fun-gi

Fun-gi is a game studio whose primary product is a mobile game House Flip, where players

- buy, renovate, and flip homes for profit

- explore diverse architectural styles along their trips.

Client Needs

Introduce their current demographic to XR technology, thus to broaden the scope of their future games

- Target regions: Australia and New Zealand

- Current Demographic: women aged 25-54 who are used to solitaire & sudoku

- Game Features: Collaborative house renovation, global tours, voting, true architecture, and empowerment.


Problem Statement

The one True Dream Home experience gives middle-aged women (25-53 years old) an opportunity to create and immerse in their dream home with their desired interior design and dream location, by introducing the use of virtual reality technology.



Competitive Analysis

Although they have the feature of home design in dream locations, there are no further features supporting for the real-time environment change, which is our leverage and advantage point.

User Interviews

Insights

- Excitement about True Dream Home's living environment changes and personalized decoration.

-Strong interest in creating and navigating within the virtual environment.

- Concerns focus on the differences between virtual and real interactions.

Implications

- Smooth VR onboarding for XR newcomers.

- Advanced interaction options: furniture customization, serene dream locations.
